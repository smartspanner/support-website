# Welcome to Smartspanner Support

Welcome to our support site for Smartspanner CMMS. Video tutorials will guide you through our software. Please note this site is still being updated!

## Getting Started

Smartspanner is a Computerized Maintenance Management System (CMMS) designed to help you manage your maintenance operations efficiently. Whether you're tracking procedures, managing checklists, handling lockouts, or monitoring asset conditions, our platform provides the tools you need.

## What You'll Find Here

Use the sidebar to navigate through the support sections and watch video tutorials on each section. If you are just getting started we recommend following each section in sequence in the getting started section, then setting up assets and creating your work orders. This will cover the basics of Smartspanner CMMS.

## Looking for More Help?

If you can't find the answers here, please contact our support team through the Smartspanner application. We're here to help you get the most out of your CMMS.
